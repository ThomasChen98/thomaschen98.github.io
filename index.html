Yuxin Chen - 陈瑀昕

Hi there! I'm a second-year Ph.D. student at the University of California, Berkeley, affiliated with the Berkeley Artificial Intelligence Research (BAIR). I have the privilege of working with Prof. Masayoshi Tomizuka, a distinguished member of National Academy of Engineering in the Mechanical Systems Control (MSC) Lab.

Prior to this, I obtained a Master's degree in Robotics advised by Prof. Ramanarayan Vasudevan and a Bachelor's degree in Aerospace Engineering supervised by Prof. Ella Atkins from the University of Michigan, Ann Arbor. I also hold a Bachelor's degree in Mechanical Engineering from Shanghai Jiao Tong University. I have also spent time in Zoox and ZF as interns.
